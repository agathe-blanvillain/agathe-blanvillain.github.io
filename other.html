Rendez-Vous des Jeunes Mathématiciennes et Informaticiennes

  • I am organising the RJMI 2024 of Inria Paris with Dounia M'Foukh and Ludovic De Matteis. They will take place in October 2024. During two days, we open Inria Paris to young girls, in high school, who are motivated by mathematics and computer science. They take part to workshops and informal discussions with mathematicians and computer scientists, so they can consider the place that these subjects can have in their studies and their future career. The aim is to encourage them to affirm their interest in mathematics and computer science and to develop an ambitious project for scientific studies.
